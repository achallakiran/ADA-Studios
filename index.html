OUR STORY

🚀 The Mission

ADA Studios — short for Alien Dimension Arcade — was born from a family's shared love of games, stories and making things together. We build games that spark imagination across generations.

🎮 Our Style

Bold characters. Sharp lines. Cartoon worlds with real heart. Every animation, every sound and every mechanic is designed with care — and a healthy dose of family input.

🌟 Est. 2024

Founded in 2024, we're just getting started. Our first titles blend Ravi's dev skills, Divya's artistic eye, Kaushiki's brilliant ideas and Kautubh's… enthusiastic chaos testing.

💌 Family First

Every game we make is a family project. More love, more laughter, more weird dinner-table ideas, and more late-night bug fixes. We wouldn't have it any other way.

THE MISSION
👾

WE BUILD FOR JOY

Games should feel alive — handcrafted and filled with personality. We believe the best games come from passion, not just process. Every ADA title is a piece of our family's soul.

Our goal: games that kids and adults play together, that spark conversations, that feel memorable even years later.
