OUR STORY

🚀 The Mission

ADA Studios — short for Alien Dimension Arcade — was born from a family's shared love of games, stories, and making things together. We build games that spark imagination and bring joy across generations.

🎮 Our Style

Bold characters. Sharp lines. Cartoon worlds with heart. We believe games should feel handcrafted and alive — every animation, every sound, every mechanic designed with care (and a lot of family input!).

🌟 Est. 2024

Founded in 2024, we're just getting started. Our first titles are in development, blending Ravi's dev skills, Divya's artistic eye, Kaushiki's brilliant ideas and Kautubh's… enthusiastic chaos testing.

💌 Family First

Every game we make is a family project. That means more love, more laughter, more weird ideas at dinner, and more late-night bug fixes. We wouldn't have it any other way.
